Milestone 4

April 15 - 19, 2024

Marketing and User Guide

This week, we focused on marketing our product. Our product trailer starts by showing the deficiencies of existing AR renovation apps and proceeds to showcase the immersive experience of our product. We highlight three key features of the app that distinguishes itself from competitors:

  1. Immersive passthrough that blends the real and virtual environments
  2. Accurate room scanning for realistic furniture placement
  3. Ability to change floor, wall, and ceiling textures
Through last week's playtesting, we found that there is still a learning curve for the controls. Users sometimes have a hard time finding the location of the buttons while wearing the headset. To smooth out the learning curve, we made a user guide that walks through the key features of our app. Given more time, we would gradually introduce each feature in the app instead of showing all of them upfront in a video.
