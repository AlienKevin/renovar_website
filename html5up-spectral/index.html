Our Mission

RemodAR lets homeowners experience their final remodeled home before the first hammer swings using Augmented Reality.


Our AR technology projects your renovation plans into your actual space, allowing you to walk through, interact with, and refine every detail until it's perfect.

Business Model

  • Market opportunity

    The U.S. residential remodeling market size was estimated at USD 527.36 billion in 2023 and is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 4.6% from 2024 to 2030. We will be targeting the mid-to-high end customers who value the benefits of visualizing their remodeling projects in detail before committing to the work. These customers are more likely to invest in technology that can save them time, money, and stress in the long run as their remodeling projects are often highly personalized, intricate, and costly.

  • Income

    We will not charge homeowner anything but will sell our service to designers who need a better way of showcasing their design and gaining customer satisfaction. We will have two- tiered pricing model where independent designers will be charged $50 a month and a team of less than 5 designers will be charged $200 a month for additional collaborative features.

  • Expenses

    The main expanse is the cost for developing the software. There are two parts to the software: one is designer facing and need to integrate with existing design frameworks and the other one is homeowner facing and need high-fidelity rendering for an appealing presentation. Our team also needs to get access to the latest headsets like Quest 3 and Vision Pro, which costs from $1,499 to $3,499. The team also needs pay for a license for the Unity game engine, which costs $2,040 per year per seat. Lastly, there is cost for setting up the company website, marketing, and general operation costs like rent.
